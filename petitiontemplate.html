Access to Water For All

Help keep the taps flowing for all Africans

Africa is facing complex water issues. While mega water users like coal-fired power plants continue to have access to water, ordinary people don’t have access to water to keep their families safe. The scarcity of water puts Africa’s people at risk.

Water is a fundamental human right: all governments must deliver safe water for all, without protecting the interests of industries that are polluting and stealing water from people.  

Take the pledge to:

 

  • Support the drive for all to have access to safe, clean water at an appropriate level and standard for all
  • Hold your government to account for the fair use, and distribution of water to the people
  • Build awareness to use water wisely in your own community
